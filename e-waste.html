About Us

Welcome to E-Waste Facility Locator, your trusted resource for finding responsible e-waste recycling facilities near you. At E-Waste Facility Locator, we understand the critical importance of properly disposing of electronic waste to protect our environment and safeguard public health. With the rapid advancement of technology, electronic devices have become an integral part of our daily lives. However, the improper disposal of these devices can have severe consequences, including pollution, resource depletion, and health hazards. Our mission is to make it easy for individuals and businesses to find reputable e-waste recycling facilities in their communities. We believe that everyone plays a vital role in promoting sustainability and reducing the environmental impact of electronic waste. By connecting users with certified recycling centers, we empower them to make responsible choices and contribute to a cleaner, greener future. E-Waste Facility Locator is committed to providing accurate, up-to-date information on e-waste recycling options across the globe. Whether you're looking to recycle old smartphones, laptops, TVs, or other electronic devices, our comprehensive database ensures that you can find the right facility to meet your needs. We collaborate with a network of certified recycling partners who adhere to the highest industry standards for environmental protection and data security. When you use E-Waste Facility Locator, you can trust that your electronic waste will be handled responsibly and ethically, minimizing its impact on the planet and supporting sustainable practices. Thank you for choosing E-Waste Facility Locator as your go-to resource for e-waste recycling. Together, we can make a difference and create a more sustainable future for generations to come. Join us in our mission to protect the planet, one electronic device at a time.
